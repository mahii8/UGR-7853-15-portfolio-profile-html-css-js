My Hobbies and Interests

Painting

Painting, especially with watercolors, is my favorite creative outlet. I love the way watercolors blend and flow, creating soft, vibrant, and unpredictable textures. Most of my inspiration comes from nature—sunsets, landscapes, and flowers often find their way onto my canvas.

Besides being an artistic expression, painting helps me unwind and enhances my focus. It’s incredibly satisfying to turn a blank page into a vibrant masterpiece, even if it’s just for myself. I believe art is not about perfection but about enjoying the process.

Classic Literature

Books have been a constant companion throughout my life, and I find particular joy in classic literature. Works like *Pride and Prejudice* by Jane Austen, *Crime and Punishment* by Fyodor Dostoevsky, and *1984* by George Orwell captivate me with their depth and insight.

Reading these classics allows me to travel through time and immerse myself in different eras, cultures, and philosophies. Beyond the stories, I enjoy analyzing the themes, characters, and societal critiques embedded in the text. Books fuel my curiosity and inspire me to write my own thoughts.

Music

Music has been an integral part of my daily life, whether I’m working, relaxing, or looking for inspiration. I enjoy exploring various genres, from the intricate harmonies of classical symphonies to the upbeat rhythms of indie and pop tracks.

Listening to music helps me connect with emotions and memories. It serves as a backdrop to both my highs and lows, making every moment more vivid. Here's one of my favorite tracks:

Wildflower - Billie Eilish
